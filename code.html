Education — Effect of starting age on school performance

Replication materials

GitHub  ·  Springer chapter

Related to the book chapter “Evaluating Educational Policies in Practice”. The chapter evaluates two education policies using empirical methods: (i) the Texas Top Ten Percent rule, and (ii) a regression discontinuity design studying school-starting age, with results suggesting older starters generally perform better in standardized tests.
